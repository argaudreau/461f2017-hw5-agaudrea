Welcome

This small webpage will compare different models of cars based on their Manufacturer's Suggested Retail Price (MSRP) and their fuel economy in miles per gallon (MPG). After inputting each vehicle's information, the cost per mile per gallon and cost of fuel over 100,000 miles will be calculated and displayed in a dynamic table.
